Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 26, 2026

Our Commitment to Privacy

This app is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information from your device. All features operate entirely on your device, with no data sent to external servers.

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect ZERO information. This app does not:

  • Collect personal information
  • Collect face data, facial geometry, or images
  • Track usage analytics
  • Send crash reports
  • Connect to external servers
  • Use cookies or tracking technologies
  • Collect advertising identifiers
  • Access your location
  • Store or transmit camera or microphone data

2. HOW THE APP WORKS

Camera Access (Eye Tracking & Facial Landmarks)

  • Purpose: The app uses your device's front camera and MediaPipe technology to detect facial landmarks and track eye movements. This allows users with limited mobility to control an on-screen pointer and interact with the app hands-free.
  • Processing: All face, facial landmark, and eye tracking processing happens strictly locally on your device in real-time using computer vision technology.
  • Storage: No camera images, video, face data, facial geometry, or eye tracking data is ever stored, saved, or transmitted.
  • Your Control: You can revoke camera permission at any time in your device settings.

Microphone Access (Speech Features)

  • Purpose: The app may request microphone access for speech-based features.
  • Processing: All voice processing happens locally on your device.
  • Storage: No audio recordings are stored or transmitted.
  • Your Control: You can revoke microphone permission at any time in your device settings.

Local Data Storage

  • The app stores user preferences, custom phrases, and calibration settings locally on your device.
  • This data never leaves your device.
  • You can delete this data by uninstalling the app or clearing app data in your device settings.

3. DATA SHARING

We share NO data with anyone. Because we don't collect any data, there is nothing to share with:

  • Third-party services
  • Advertisers
  • Analytics companies
  • Other users
  • Anyone else

4. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY (COPPA & FERPA COMPLIANCE)

This app is safe for children and students of all ages:

  • No data collection: We don't collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.
  • Educational use: This app is designed for use in educational settings and complies with FERPA (Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act).
  • Parental control: Parents and educators have full control over the app through device-level permissions.
  • No third parties: Children's data is never shared because no data is collected.

5. INTERNATIONAL USERS (GDPR COMPLIANCE)

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), UK, and Switzerland:

  • Data protection by design: This app is built with privacy as the foundation.
  • Your rights: Because we don't collect data, there is no data to access, correct, delete, or transfer.
  • No consent needed: We don't need your consent to collect data because we don't collect any.
  • Legal basis: Not applicable - no processing of personal data occurs.

6. U.S. STATE PRIVACY RIGHTS

For residents of California, Virginia, Colorado, Connecticut, and other states with privacy laws:

7. ACCESSIBILITY & ASSISTIVE TECHNOLOGY

This app is designed as an assistive communication tool:
