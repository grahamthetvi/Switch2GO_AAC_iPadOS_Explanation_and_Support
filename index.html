Designed for Independence

Switch2Go makes communication accessible with customizable features tailored for unique visual and physical needs.

👁️

CVI-Optimized Interface

Configure layouts with 2-4 symbols per page to reduce visual complexity. Customize high-contrast colors and text sizes to accommodate specific visual needs.

✨

Multimodal User Interface

Uses ARCore to track head movements and eye gaze technology, enabling completely hands-free operation. Users can navigate and select items simply by lingering their gaze.

💬

Saved Phrases

Access a curated list of common phrases or create entirely custom ones with personalized appearance settings for quick communication in daily routines.

🔌

(Upcoming) Switch Control

Support for Arduino-based USB switch controls, mapping physical switch presses directly to customized phrase tiles without complex bluetooth pairing.

🛡️

100% Private

All face tracking, eye tracking, and voice processing happens completely locally on your device. Zero data collection, absolute privacy.

📱

Cross-Platform

Available on both Android and iOS with 100% feature parity. Built with a modern Kotlin Multiplatform architecture.
